Website Planning Document
Sabargata Sunni Momin Welfare Society
This document outlines the recommended structure, features, and design ideas for the official website of Sabargata Sunni Momin Welfare Society.
1. Home Page
•	Society logo and tagline
•	Banner with community service photos
•	Quick action buttons: Donate, Become Member, Apply for Help, Contact Us
•	Sections for About, Activities, Events, Gallery, and Community Statistics
2. About Us
•	History of the society
•	Vision and Mission
•	Founder details
•	Trust registration information
•	Major achievements timeline
3. Trustees / Committee Members
•	Photos and details of President, Trustees, Secretary, Treasurer, and committee members
•	Short introduction and designation for each member
4. Welfare Activities
•	Education Support
•	Medical Help
•	Marriage Assistance
•	Relief Work
•	Employment & Skill Development
5. Donation Section
•	UPI QR code and bank details
•	Online payment gateway
•	Donation categories
•	Transparency through receipts and reports
6. Membership / Join Us
•	Membership registration form
•	Volunteer registration
•	Youth participation section
7. Help Request Section
•	Forms for educational, medical, and emergency assistance requests
8. News & Announcements
•	Event notices
•	Scholarship updates
•	Community announcements
•	Festival greetings
9. Photo & Video Gallery
•	Albums of welfare activities, events, and community programs
10. Contact Page
•	Office address
•	Google Maps integration
•	Contact numbers and email
•	WhatsApp contact button
11. Languages
•	Gujarati and English language support
12. Professional Features
•	Mobile-friendly design
•	Admin panel for updates
•	Future member login system
13. Recommended Design Style
•	Elegant and trustworthy color scheme
•	Dark Green, White, Gold accents
•	Clean and modern layout
14. Social Media Integration
•	Instagram
•	Facebook
•	YouTube
•	WhatsApp Channel
15. Technical Recommendation
•	Use WordPress for easy management and updates
16. Community Achievement Section
•	Highlight achievers such as Doctors, Engineers, CA, Government Officers, Toppers, and Entrepreneurs

The website should reflect transparency, professionalism, community service, and modern communication while remaining simple and accessible for all users.
